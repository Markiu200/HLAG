Grand Full Access:
Add-MailboxPermission -Identity <alias> -User <user> -AccessRights FullAccess -AutoMapping:$false -Confirm:$false

Remove Full Access:
Remove-MailboxPermission -Identity <alias> -User '<user>' -AccessRights 'FullAccess' -Confirm:$false

Grand Send on Behalf:
Set-Mailbox '<alias>' -Grantsendonbehalfto @{add="<user>"}

Remove Send on Behalf:
Set-Mailbox '<alias>' -Grantsendonbehalfto @{remove="<user>"} -Confirm:$false

Grand Send As (onprem):
Get-Mailbox "<alias>" | Add-ADPermission -User "<user>" -ExtendedRights "Send As"

Grand Send As (O365):
Add-RecipientPermission <alias> -Trustee <user> -AccessRights "SendAs" -confirm:$false

Remove Send As (onprem):
Get-Mailbox "<alias>" | Remove-ADPermission -User "<user>" -ExtendedRights "Send As"

Remove Send As (O365):
Remove-RecipientPermission <alias> -Trustee <user> -AccessRights "SendAs" -confirm:$false

Add member to Distribution List:
Add-DistributionGroupMember -Identity <alias> -Member  "<user>" -confirm:$false

Remove member from Distribution List:
Remove-DistributionGroupMember -Identity <alias> -Member  "<user>" -confirm:$false

Add AD group:
Add-ADGroupMember -Identity <group> -Members  "<user>" -confirm:$false

Remove AD group:
Remove-ADGroupMember -Identity <group> -Member  "<user>" -confirm:$false

Create room:
Enable-RemoteMailbox "gotcornerroom" -RemoteRoutingAddress "gotcornerroom@hlag.mail.onmicrosoft.com" -Alias "gotcornerroom" -Room

ID to UPN:
get-aduser <id> -properties * | Select-Object UserPrincipalName -ExpandProperty UserPrincipalName >> results.txt

UPN to ID:
Get-ADUser -Filter "UserPrincipalName -eq '<upn>'" -Properties SamAccountNAme | Select-Object -ExpandProperty SamAccountName >> results.txt
