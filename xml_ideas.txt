Instead of having to write everything in HTML, XML markup can be used to generate pages.


For articles
<article>
<\article>

For checklists
<checklist>
    <step>
        <title>Step 1</title>
        <instructions>Plain text...<\instructions>
    <\step>
    ...
    <step>
        <title>Step N</title>
        <instructions link="filename"><\instructions>
    <\step>
<\checklist>
